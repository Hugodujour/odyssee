About us

We are Jean and Matthieu Compeyrot, the founders of Odyssée. In collaboration with other passionate winemakers, we create exceptional wines. Odyssée represents much more than just a brand; it is the result of our commitment to environmentally friendly agricultural practices and our desire to provide you with a unique experience through our exceptional cuvées.

Our ecological practices

At Odyssée, we are firmly committed to ecological practices. Our sustainable business model is built on environmentally friendly viticulture methods, reducing our carbon footprint, and preserving local ecosystems. Furthermore, we maintain strong partnerships with like-minded winemakers, creating a community dedicated to the preservation of our planet.
